Cultivation

On the Sankey diagram, we focus on three attributes: catch rate, species and growth rate (from the left column to the right column). Users can get the information of the catch rate and growth rate of different species. The left column is the catch rate, we divide it into 5 degrees, when catch rate ≤ 50, it belongs to "Very Easy" section; when 50 < catch rate ≤ 100, it belongs to "Easy" section; when 100 < catch rate ≤ 150, it belongs to "Middle" section; when 150 < catch rate ≤ 200, it belongs to "Hard" section; when catch rate ≥ 200, it belongs to "Super Hard" section. And the growth rate has 6 levels: 'Slow', 'Medium Slow','Medium Fast', 'Fast', 'Erratic' and 'Fluctuating'. When the mouse hovers over the link, it will tell the count number of Pokémons belonging to each level, so that we can easily find out which kind of species is easier to catch and faster in growing.
