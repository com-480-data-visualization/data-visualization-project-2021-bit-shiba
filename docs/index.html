Pokémon shortened from Pocket Monster, is any of the documented species of the organisms that have evolved and inhabited the fictional “Pokémon World” with an innate connection to element-based supernatural powers. Officially, Pokémon is a Japanese media franchise managed by The Pokémon Company, a company founded by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures. This year is in commemoration of the twenty-fifth anniversary of the Pokémon franchise, which began with the original Japanese release of Pocket Monsters Red and Green for the Game Boy on February 27, 1996.
