Pokeball

This bubble diagram displays as Poke Balls to exhibit different types of Pokémons. When selecting a Poke Ball, you can see detailed information of a type of Pokémons, such as the name, the number of each type of Pokémons, the typical abilities, appearance description, and skills assessment. Under this text description, a typical image of each type follows. Selecting Pokémon through Poké Balls simulates the process of catching and storing Pokémon, which will let users feel the Pokémon world, and gain a better understanding of the legendary Pocket Monsters. Combining the words and pictures, you can use a fast, easy and vivid way to open your own Poke Balls, and choose the preferred type.
