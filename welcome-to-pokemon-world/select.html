Here, we give a visualization to compare different Pokemon and inspire you to choose your admired Pokemon. In this section, you just need to input the Pokedex of Pokemon and then you can see two or three different pokemon with their species strength, appearance, radar chart of 6 base abilities (HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed). Also, you can check thier Evolutions and appearance changes. Some attached information such as the Pokemon's generation and its discovered area can also be found here. After the comparasion and comprehensive consideration, you can draw a conclusion to find your own admired Pokemon!
